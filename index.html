Where accessibility meets fun — and fun creates inclusion.

Transforming Team Dynamics Through Gamified Accessibility

We bridge the gap between accessibility and engagement through immersive virtual escape rooms designed for inclusive corporate team‑building. Our approach treats accessibility not as a technical hurdle, but as a competitive advantage.

By integrating gamification into our virtual escape rooms, we create high‑stakes environments where diverse team members must collaborate, communicate, and solve challenges together. This fosters an inclusive culture where every voice is heard and every skill is valued.

Designed for Everyone

Each escape room is delivered online, ensuring that all team members can participate regardless of disability. The experience is intentionally crafted to be enjoyable, engaging, and fully inclusive.

Our content is built using best‑practice accessibility principles, ensuring that accessibility is not an afterthought — it is the foundation.

Expert‑Led Accessibility Content

The accessibility challenges within our escape rooms are based on the combined expertise of two of the world’s leading senior document accessibility specialists. Jon and Sam have written formal training programmes, delivered hundreds of accessibility awareness sessions, and presented at Naidex 2026.

They are highly sought‑after public speakers on accessibility, regularly presenting at events for public sector organisations and global audiences.

Our 2026 Escape Room Themes

Each escape room is built around a high‑stakes scenario that demands teamwork, communication, and inclusive problem‑solving. Every theme is designed to challenge assumptions, highlight accessibility barriers, and reward teams who collaborate effectively.

❄️ Antarctica: Survival on the Ice

Your team is stranded at an Antarctic research station after a systems failure. With temperatures dropping and communication equipment failing, you must work together to restore power, decode emergency protocols, and coordinate a rescue.

This room emphasises clarity, shared responsibility, and accessible communication under pressure.

🚀 Apollo 13: Critical Mission Control

Inspired by the real Apollo 13 crisis, your team steps into Mission Control as a spacecraft suffers catastrophic damage. Every instruction must be precise, every decision inclusive, and every voice heard.

This room highlights the importance of accessible documentation, structured communication, and cross‑functional teamwork.

🔒 Alcatraz: The Impossible Escape

Set inside the world’s most infamous prison, your team must navigate a series of puzzles, hidden clues, and accessibility‑based challenges to break free before lockdown.

This room focuses on problem‑solving, alternative perspectives, and the value of diverse thinking styles.

🛰️ International Space Station: Systems Failure

Orbiting 250 miles above Earth, your team faces a cascading systems failure aboard the ISS. With limited time and limited resources, you must stabilise life‑support, restore navigation, and coordinate with ground control.

This room reinforces accessible teamwork, calm decision‑making, and inclusive communication in high‑pressure environments.
